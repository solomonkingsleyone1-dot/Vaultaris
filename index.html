Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common investment questions

What is the minimum investment amount?

We offer flexible investment plans starting from just $50! Whether you're starting small or investing significant amounts, we provide personalized strategies tailored to your budget and goals. For optimal diversification and automated portfolio management, we recommend $500+.

What are typical 7-day returns?

Our average 7-day return across all portfolios is +1.7%, with daily variations. Returns are calculated based on real market performance and our AI-driven strategies. Use our 7-day returns calculator to estimate potential gains for your investment amount.

Note: Past performance doesn't guarantee future results. Investment involves risk.

How are your investment strategies different?

Our strategies combine quantitative analysis with AI-powered market predictions. We focus on long-term growth while implementing dynamic risk management that adjusts to market conditions in real-time.

What fees do you charge?

We operate on a transparent fee structure: 1.5% annual management fee on assets under management. No hidden fees, no performance fees. We only succeed when you succeed.

How often will I receive performance reports?

Monthly detailed reports via email, quarterly comprehensive reviews, and 24/7 access to your personal dashboard with real-time performance tracking.

Is my investment secured and insured?

All client assets are held with top-tier custodians (like Fidelity or Charles Schwab) who provide SIPC insurance up to $500,000. We never hold client funds directly.

Can I withdraw my funds at any time?

Yes, we offer liquidity with no lock-up periods. Withdrawal requests are processed within 3-5 business days. Certain investment products may have specific timelines.
